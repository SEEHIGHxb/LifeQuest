# Privacy & Data Statement

_Last updated: 2026-07-14_

Life Balance Index is a **local-first** personal wellbeing tool. It has no
account system, no server, and no database. This statement explains exactly
what happens to your data.

## What data the app holds

When you use the app you may enter demographic details, income and financial
habits, physical-activity and sleep data, and answers to validated
psychological screeners (well-being, stress, loneliness, social network,
self-efficacy, grit, and others). All of this is **assessment data about you**.

## Where it is stored

- Everything is stored **only in your browser's `localStorage`, on your own
  device.** It is never transmitted to us or to any third-party server,
  because the app has no backend to send it to.
- There is **no analytics, no tracking, and no telemetry.** We do not collect
  usage data, crash reports, or identifiers.

## When data leaves your device

Your data only leaves your device when **you** deliberately export or share it:

- **Export backup** produces a JSON file that you save wherever you choose. It
  contains your full local data. Keep it somewhere you trust.
- **Comparison Codes** (the peer-comparison feature) encode **only** your
  display name, level, points, and the eight 0–100 aspect scores — never your
  raw questionnaire answers, income, or health details. You choose who to
  share a code with.

## Third parties

- **Google Fonts** are currently loaded from Google's CDN. When the app loads,
  your IP address is visible to Google as part of that request. No app data is
  sent to Google. (Self-hosting the fonts to remove this is a tracked
  improvement.)
- No other third-party services are used.

## Deleting your data

- **Reset Data** in the app clears your local save.
- Clearing your browser's site data for this page removes everything.
- Because nothing is stored off your device, deletion is complete and
  immediate — there are no server copies to request removal of.

## Thailand PDPA note

Under Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), a "data controller" is an
entity that collects, uses, or discloses personal data. Life Balance Index does
**not** collect your assessment data: it is processed locally, on your device,
under your sole control, and is never received by the developer. You remain in
control of any backup file or Comparison Code you choose to export or share.

## Not medical advice

The app administers screening instruments (e.g. WHO-5, ST-5) for self-insight.
It is **not a diagnosis and not a substitute for professional care.** If your
results suggest distress, please contact a professional — in Thailand, the
Department of Mental Health hotline is **1323** (free, 24 hours).
